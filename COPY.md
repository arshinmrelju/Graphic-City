## 5. FAQ

**What does a project typically cost?**
> Every project is different, but most engagements fall between $25,000 and $150,000 depending on scope, timeline, and complexity. We'll give you a clear estimate after our first conversation.

**How long does a brand identity take?**
> A full brand identity — strategy through delivery — typically takes 8 to 12 weeks. Digital projects vary based on scope. We'll map out the timeline together before we begin.

**Do you work with early-stage startups?**
> We do. Some of our best work has been with companies in their earliest days. We also work with established organizations. Size matters less than ambition.

**What if we're not ready to commit to a full engagement?**
> We offer discovery sessions — a half-day workshop that defines your needs, your direction, and a plan forward. No commitment required beyond the session itself.

**Do you offer rush timelines?**
> Occasionally, for the right project. We do not compromise on quality for speed. If a timeline is aggressive, we will tell you honestly what is possible — and what is not.

**What do you need from us to get started?**
> Your time, your honesty, and your willingness to be challenged. The best work comes from genuine partnership. We bring the process, the craft, and the rigor. You bring your vision and your trust.

**Can we see examples in our industry?**
> We can share relevant case studies during our initial conversation. We do not publish all of our work publicly — some of our best partnerships are built on confidentiality.

**How do we know if we're a good fit?**
> We schedule a conversation. No pitch, no pressure. We ask questions, you ask questions. If there's alignment, we move forward. If not, we'll be the first to tell you.

## 20. Voice Principles

1. **Declare, don't describe.** You never need to say something is good if you show it well.

2. **Prefer short words.** "Use" over "utilize." "Show" over "demonstrate." "Help" over "facilitate."

3. **One idea per sentence.** Short sentences build trust. Long sentences build confusion.

4. **Answer the question behind the question.** When someone reads "What we do," they are really asking "Can you help me?" Address that.

5. **Generosity without apology.** We share our thinking freely. We never apologize for our process or our prices.

6. **No filler.** Every paragraph, every sentence, every word must earn its place. If it can be removed, remove it.

7. **Warmth through precision, not informality.** We do not use casual language to seem friendly. We are precise because we respect the reader's time. That is how we show warmth.

---

*Tone is not a switch you flip. It is a series of consistent choices. Every piece of copy on every surface should feel like it was written by the same person, for the same person: someone who cares deeply about quality and has no tolerance for waste.*
